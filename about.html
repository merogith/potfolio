A bit about me

The longer version: where I come from and what I work on.

I'm Meriç. I've always wanted to know how things work, whether that's a machine, a business, or the way people make decisions. That's the thread behind most of what I do.

I studied environmental engineering at METU in Ankara, then moved to Berlin for a master's on the business and finance side. They're two fairly different worlds, and most of my work since has sat somewhere between them.

What connects them is data. A business shows up in its numbers. Money shows up in how people behave around it. A game is a set of rules whose effects you can measure. My master's thesis was about people too: how they decide whether to trust a new technology. I like work that has both a measurable side and a human side, and I try not to lose sight of either.

Day to day I'm a data and business-intelligence analyst. I take data that's messy, incomplete, or sometimes just qualitative, work out what matters in it, and turn that into something someone can act on: a clear report, a chart that's worth showing, a recommendation that's honest about what it doesn't know. I care more about the decision than the dashboard. I'm not a data engineer or an ML engineer and won't pretend to be; what I'm good at is the analysis and the judgement around it.

I also build things. On the side I've shipped a few browser games, properly tested and with real engines behind them. It's a useful reminder of how much harder it is to build software than to talk about it. At work I often end up as the link between the technical side and everyone else; I'm comfortable sitting with an engineer and a non-technical colleague on the same day and helping them understand each other.

Outside work, I coach people one-to-one on their relationship with money. It comes from the same place: helping someone feel more in control of something that tends to cause a lot of stress. (More on the coaching →)

Right now I'm looking for data, BI, and analytics work across the EU or remote, ideally somewhere that values careful thinking and doesn't mind a new question. I'm in Berlin, authorised to work across the EU, and available to start now.

— Meriç
