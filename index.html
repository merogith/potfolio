About

I'm Meriç. I've always been curious about how things work — not just machines, but people and the systems they build together. That curiosity is the thread behind everything here.

It took me from environmental engineering at METU to a master's in Corporate Management in Berlin, and into work across equity research, pharmaceutical data, and early-stage edtech. Financial coaching

On the side, I coach people one to one on their money. Most arrive feeling anxious or unsure where to begin, and leave understanding their own finances well enough to make their own calls. It's education and habit-building, in plain language.

  • Understand your money. Where the worry and the habits actually come from.
  • Learn the basics. Budgeting, saving, and how investing really works.
  • Build a plan you'll stick to, with regular check-ins while the habit forms.

Coaching and education, not regulated financial advice. I don't sell products or pick your investments. Sessions in English or Turkish.
