About

I'm a data analyst based in Berlin who likes turning messy, real-world data into decisions people actually act on. My path is a little unusual: I started in environmental engineering at METU, moved into business and finance with an M.Sc. in Berlin, and along the way became the person teams rely on to make sense of their data — CRM records across 70+ countries, equity valuations, and product analytics for an early-stage startup.

The thread connecting all of it is the same: quantitative analysis that drives a real decision — whether the units are dollars, prescriptions, or µS/cm. I also build: I ship tested, production-quality browser games on the side, which keeps me close to the engineering that analysis usually only describes.

Lately I'm especially interested in how organizations actually adopt AI — the subject of my master's thesis and a paper currently under review. Work-authorized across the EU and available immediately.
