About

I'm Meriç, a data and business-intelligence analyst in Berlin. I like taking something complicated and working out what's going on inside it.

My background is mixed: environmental engineering at METU, then a business and finance master's in Berlin, and work across equity research, pharmaceutical data, and early-stage edtech. The common thread is the same every time: take the data, work out what matters, and make it clear enough that someone can decide what to do.
