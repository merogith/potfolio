Analyst & builder · Berlin

I dig into messy problems and work out what to do about them.

Most of my work is analysis — in finance, pharma, and gaming. I take data that's messy or half-missing, work out what actually matters, and turn it into a clear call someone can make. I also build browser games from scratch, which scratches the same itch from the other side: watching how a system behaves once real people get hold of it.

It's the same instinct every time: find the structure under the mess and make it clear enough to act on — whether the system is a market, a sales pipeline, or a game I built to watch it behave. The range isn't the point; it's the evidence.
