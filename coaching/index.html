Is this for you?

My approach

How I think about money coaching

This is coaching and education, not advice. The aim isn't a hot tip; it's that you understand your money well enough to make your own decisions.

We start with you

We begin with how you feel and behave around money, because that's usually where budgets fall apart.

Education, in plain language

Budgeting, saving, and how investing works, explained so it sticks. No hype, no jargon.

Ongoing, not one-off

Not a single lecture. Regular check-ins and a plan that changes as your life does.

My job isn't to hand you a winning bet. It's to help you understand your options and your own habits well enough to make good decisions, and to stick around while you put them into practice.

How it works

What working together looks like

A free intro call

Half an hour on where you are and what you'd like to change. No pitch. If I'm not the right fit for you, I'll say so.

A plan that fits you

Built around your situation, budget, and goals rather than a template. We agree together what "better" looks like for you.

A weekly rhythm

Short weekly check-ins and online sessions, tailored to how much support you want. I'll flag things when they're worth a look.

Steady support between sessions

A newsletter, the occasional "have you thought about this?", and someone to ask when you're not sure.

What to expect

How working together usually goes

No magic, no stock tips. Most of the change is small and steady: you start looking at your money instead of avoiding it, the basics stop feeling intimidating, and decisions get easier because you understand what's behind them.

Early on

We get clear on where things stand and what you want to change. Often just looking at it honestly is a relief in itself.

Along the way

You learn the fundamentals at your own pace, and we turn them into habits that fit your real life rather than an ideal one.

Over time

Money becomes something you manage on purpose instead of worry about. You can make your own calls and explain why.

Questions

The things people ask first

What does it cost?

It depends on what you need and what you can manage, so I sort it out on the free intro call rather than putting a fixed price here. There's no obligation to continue.

Which languages do you work in?

English or Turkish, whichever you think more clearly in. Money is emotional, and it helps to talk about it in your own language.

Is this financial advice?

No. It's coaching and education. I won't tell you which fund to buy or pick your investments. I help you understand your options and your own habits so you can decide well. If you need regulated advice, I'll tell you, and point you to a licensed adviser.

Where do sessions happen, and how often?

Online, wherever you are. Usually weekly, adjusted to how much support you want, with more at the start and less as things settle.

Do I need to be "good with money" already?

Not at all. Most people come because they're not, and that's fine. We start exactly where you are, without judgement.
